Health Guidelines & Preventive Measures

Hand Hygiene

Wash hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after being in public places.

Wear Masks

Use masks in crowded settings, especially in areas with high transmission rates.

Social Distancing

Maintain at least 6 feet distance from others in public spaces to reduce transmission risk.

Vaccination

Stay up-to-date with recommended vaccines to protect against preventable diseases.
